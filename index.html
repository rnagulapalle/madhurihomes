The approach

Small caseload. Long view.

Most agents optimize for volume. I optimize for fit. I take on a handful of clients at a time so that when something new hits the market that matches you, you hear from me directly, within hours.

That pace also means I can be honest. If a listing isn't right for you, I'll tell you. If now isn't the right time to sell, I'll tell you that too. The math works because trust compounds — most of my clients come from other clients.
